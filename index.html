Peer Listening

Welcome to Bard Peer Listening! Feel free to call email or contact us anonymously via the chat on this page. Please note if there is a risk of harming yourself or other you will be reffered to a guidance counsler.

What Is It?

This peer listening platform is designed for students who want an anonymous space to ask questions or discuss situations that might be harder to do in person.

How Does It Work?

This space is administered by peers with the ultimate goal of creating an open environment for healthy conversation without judgement.
